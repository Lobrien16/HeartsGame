This is Ness

Ness is a boy from a small town and he loves to help his friends. In this game, it is your job to help Ness help others. The controls are simple:

Move Ness around with the arrow keys!

Use the spacebar to interact with objects and people!

Sometimes when someone is asking for help, they'll say something to what they say! If you get stuck, the key is reading and comprehending.
And that's all you need to know to play! Have fun brightening everyones day!
